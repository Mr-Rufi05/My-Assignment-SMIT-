BEEF BIRYANI

Beef biryani is a popular dish of Indian and South Asian cuisine

that features tender beef, basmati rice, and a blend of aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg cubed Bonless Beef.
  • 25 gm Cooking Oil.
  • 150 gm Yoghurt.
  • 100 gm Mint Leaves.
  • 50 gm Pure Ghee.
  • 100 gm Crispy Fried Onions.
  • 3 gm Saffron.
  • 15 ml Rose Water.

Marinade

  • 75 gm Minced Ginger.
  • 75 gm Minced Garlic.
  • 50 gm Kashmiri Chilli Powder.
  • 30 gm Turmeric Powder.
  • 25 gm Garam Masala Powder.
  • 25 gm Green Chilli.
  • 60 ml Lime Juice.
  • Salt-to-Taste.

Method

  1. Mix the marinade in a large dish. Add Beef cubes & leave covered in a cool place for 2 hours.
  2. Cook this meat in oil for 15 mins. Add yoghurt & cook till the meat is tender. Remove from fire.
  3. Wash rice and boil with required salt till 3/4th cooked. Drain and keep aside.
  4. Assemble the meat and rice mixture in a pot. Drizzle in hot ghee. Sprinkle saffron, mint leaves from top, cover with foil & put on hotplate and cook for 20 minutes.
  5. Drizzle in the rose water and serve hot garnished with fried onion and mint leaves.
